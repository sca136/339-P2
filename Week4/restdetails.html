Café Medina

780 Richards Street, Vancouver, BC

Since 2008, we have been serving up its signature brand of Mediterranean-inspired cuisine, connoisseur-savvy coffee and sweet sustenance via the city’s best Belgian waffles. A quaint, bistro-styled restaurant replete with old-world charm and proprietor Robbie Kane’s determined philosophy of enlightened hospitality, Café Medina provides a full complement of fresh and eclectic breakfast, lunch and daily brunch dishes by Chef Jonathan Chovancek and a pioneering attitude towards café culture. Since moving to its new location in Vancouver’s Library District in August 2014, the new Café Medina has garnered numerous rave reviews, cementing its longstanding reputation as the city’s leading brunch.
